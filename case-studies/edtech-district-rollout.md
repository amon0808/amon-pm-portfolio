# 📚 Case Study: EdTech Platform District-Wide Rollout

> **Status: Full write-up coming soon**

## Overview

**Sector:** K-12 EdTech
**Scope:** District-wide platform migration and digital transformation
**Team Size:** 12 cross-functional team members
**Duration:** 8 months
**Outcome:** 20% improvement in regional performance metrics; 100% audit readiness maintained

---

## The Challenge

A large K-12 school district was operating on fragmented legacy workflows across 7 school sites. Manual processes created reporting lags, compliance risks, and inconsistent data. Leadership needed a unified platform rollout without disrupting the academic calendar.

---

## The Approach

Applied Scrum-based project execution across all 7 sites with:
- Phased rollout by site (2 sites per sprint cycle)
- Change management champions at each school
- Weekly standups with principals and district admins
- Real-time risk register updated every sprint

---

## Key Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Staff migrated to new platform | 50+ |
| Performance metric improvement | +20% |
| Audit compliance | 100% maintained |
| On-time delivery | All milestones hit |
| User adoption rate | 90%+ within 60 days |

---

## Lessons Learned

1. Principal buy-in is the #1 predictor of adoption success
2. Phased rollouts outperform big-bang deployments in K-12
3. Dedicated hypercare support in first 30 days is non-negotiable
